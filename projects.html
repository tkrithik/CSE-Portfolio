Python COVID Escape Room

My

Course: CSE (Computer Science Essentials)

Duration: Approximately 3 weeks (End of March 2021 - Mid April 2021)

Type of Project: Team Project


The objective of this project was to create an interactive escape room, themed around COVID-19, where the user can “escape” by interacting with the program through typed commands. However, this entire project must be done without the use of any loops. My team and I addressed the client’s request, the project criteria, and created an escape room using Replit, where the user had to both use their general knowledge and factual skills, along with their computer science skills. The user of our game needed to solve problems as simple as word searches, and as hard as converting binary to ASCII. Click Learn More to view more details!

App Development: Creative Expression

My

Course: CSE (Computer Science Essentials)

Duration: Approximately 4 weeks (October 2020 - Mid November 2020)

Type of Project: Team Project


The objective of this project was to develop a user-friendly app revolving around a current news topic. This app either needed to be an interactive game, an interactive story, or an interactive media page. My team and I addressed the client’s request, the project criteria, and developed an interactive user story, which was themed around the news topic of COVID-19. The user of our game needed to solve ethical and choice-making problems, such as deciding whether to wear a mask when going outside, or washing your food after you buy it. Using MIT App Inventor, we developed our app to be as user-friendly as possible. Click Learn More to view more details!
